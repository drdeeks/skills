# Ideation Techniques

## Brainstorming Methods

### 1. SCAMPER Method
- **Substitute** — What can be replaced?
- **Combine** — What can be merged?
- **Adapt** — What can be adapted?
- **Modify** — What can be changed?
- **Put to other use** — What else can it do?
- **Eliminate** — What can be removed?
- **Reverse** — What if it worked backwards?

### 2. Six Thinking Hats
1. White — Facts and data
2. Red — Emotions and feelings
3. Black — Caution and risk
4. Yellow — Benefits and optimism
5. Green — Creativity and alternatives
6. Blue — Process and control

### 3. Random Word Association
Pick a random word and force connections to your problem to spark new ideas.

## Software-Specific Techniques

- **Rubber Duck Debugging** — Explain the problem to an inanimate object
- **Worst Possible Idea** — Generate terrible solutions to break mental blocks
- **Timeboxing** — Set strict time limits for ideation phases

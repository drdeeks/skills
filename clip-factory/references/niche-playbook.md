# Niche Playbook

## Niche Tiers by Revenue Model

### Tier 1: Streamer Clips (Highest Direct Payout)
- **RPM**: $1-$5 per 1,000 views
- **Targets**: Adin Ross, N3on, Sneako, Kai Cenat, IShowSpeed, xQc, Clavicular
- **Platforms**: TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels
- **Volume**: 5-10 clips/day per creator
- **Monetization**: Content Rewards campaigns, direct creator payouts, platform RPM
- **Risk**: High competition, creator drama can tank a niche overnight

### Tier 2: Podcast Clips (Evergreen, Stable)
- **RPM**: $0.50-$3
- **Targets**: Joe Rogan, Lex Fridman, Diary of a CEO, Theo Von, Shawn Ryan, Andrew Huberman
- **Platforms**: YouTube Shorts, TikTok, X, LinkedIn (for business pods)
- **Volume**: 3-5 clips/day per podcast
- **Monetization**: Platform RPM, brand deals, affiliate links in bio
- **Risk**: Low — content is evergreen, audience is stable

### Tier 3: Hustle/Finance (Highest Brand-Deal CPM)
- **CPM**: $1.50-$8 on Content Rewards
- **Targets**: Alex Hormozi, Iman Gadzhi, Hamza, Chris Williamson
- **Platforms**: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn
- **Volume**: 3-5 clips/day
- **Monetization**: Content Rewards, brand sponsorships, affiliate programs
- **Risk**: Audience fatigue if content is repetitive

### Tier 4: Reddit Story / Fake Text (Easiest to Mass Produce)
- **RPM**: $0.30-$1.50
- **Targets**: Reddit threads, AI-generated story content
- **Platforms**: TikTok, YouTube Shorts
- **Volume**: 10-20 clips/day (low effort per clip)
- **Monetization**: Pure volume play — platform RPM
- **Risk**: Low barrier = high competition, platform crackdowns

### Tier 5: Educational / How-To
- **RPM**: $1-$4
- **Targets**: Tech tutorials, cooking, fitness, DIY
- **Platforms**: YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok
- **Volume**: 3-5 clips/day
- **Monetization**: Affiliate links, brand deals, course upsells
- **Risk**: Moderate — requires domain knowledge for hook quality

## Niche Selection Criteria (for Analytics Engine)

Score each niche 1-5 on:
1. **View velocity** — How fast do clips in this niche accumulate views in the first 24h?
2. **RPM ceiling** — What's the maximum revenue per 1,000 views?
3. **Content supply** — How much raw long-form content is available per week?
4. **Competition density** — How many active clippers already serve this niche?
5. **Monetization diversity** — How many revenue streams exist (platform RPM, Content Rewards, direct deals)?
6. **Evergreen score** — Does clip relevance decay in hours, days, or weeks?

**Composite score** = (view_velocity * 0.25) + (rpm_ceiling * 0.20) + (content_supply * 0.15) + ((6 - competition_density) * 0.15) + (monetization_diversity * 0.15) + (evergreen_score * 0.10)

## Posting Schedule Optimization

### Peak posting windows (US audience, all times CDT):
- **Morning**: 7:00-9:00 AM
- **Lunch**: 11:30 AM-1:00 PM
- **Evening**: 5:00-7:00 PM
- **Late night**: 9:00-11:00 PM

### Platform-specific cadence:
- **TikTok**: 3-5 posts/day, spaced 3+ hours apart
- **YouTube Shorts**: 2-3 posts/day
- **Instagram Reels**: 2-3 posts/day
- **X (Twitter)**: 3-5 posts/day (clips + text hooks)
- **LinkedIn**: 1-2 posts/day (business/finance niches only)

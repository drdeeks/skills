## Social Media Channels

### Facebook Groups (B2C — Expat Communities)

**Why:** Largest concentration of expat remittance senders. Groups like "Mexicanos en USA" have 500K+ members.

**Tactics:**
1. Join 10-15 relevant groups per corridor
2. Engage authentically for 1-2 weeks before any promotional content
3. Share value-first content: fee comparison graphics, "how I saved $X" stories
4. Respond to posts asking about money transfer methods
5. Build relationship with group admins — offer exclusive demo/referral code
6. Post 2-3x/week per group (not spammy, value-driven)

**Content types:** Fee comparison infographics, short video demos, testimonial quotes, "did you know" stats

**Metrics:** Group joins, post engagement rate, DM conversations started, referral link clicks

**Rules:** Never spam. Always disclose affiliation. Lead with value, not sales pitch. Follow group rules.

### Instagram (B2C — Visual storytelling)

**Tactics:**
1. Create carousel posts: "Western Union vs Email Remittance Pro" comparisons
2. Stories: 15-second demo walkthroughs
3. Reels: 30-60s "remittance hack" content
4. Hashtag strategy: #sendmoneyhome #remittance #expat #OFW #diaspora
5. Engage with expat lifestyle accounts and community pages
6. Run targeted ads ($5-20/day) to expat demographics in key corridors

**Content cadence:** 3 feed posts/week, 5 stories/week, 2 reels/week

### TikTok (B2C — Viral potential)

**Tactics:**
1. Create "money hack" style videos showing fee savings
2. Side-by-side comparisons: traditional remittance vs our flow
3. "Watch me send $100 to Mexico for $1.50" live demos
4. Collaborate with expat/immigrant content creators
5. Use trending sounds with remittance-relevant overlays

**Content types:** Demos, comparisons, testimonials, "storytime" format

**Target hashtags:** #moneytransfer #remittance #sendmoneyhome #expathack #immigrantlife

### Twitter/X (B2B + Crypto community)

**Tactics:**
1. Thread campaigns: "Why crypto remittance is broken and how we fix it"
2. Engage with DAO governance discussions about contributor payouts
3. Quote tweet traditional remittance companies' announcements with fee comparisons
4. Build relationships with crypto fintech influencers
5. Participate in Twitter Spaces about remittance/fintech

## Direct Messaging Channels

### WhatsApp (Highest conversion for B2C)

**Why:** Dominant messaging app in most remittance corridors. Workers share links and recommendations organically.

**Tactics:**
1. Create a "Remittance Tips" broadcast list for interested users
2. Share demo videos as WhatsApp statuses
3. Partner with community leaders who can forward info to their networks
4. Create shareable WhatsApp stickers with brand/fee comparison
5. Set up WhatsApp Business for customer support

**Viral mechanic:** When someone sends a remittance, the claim email includes "Share this with friends who send money home" + referral link.

### Telegram (B2C workers + B2B crypto)

**Tactics:**
1. Join expat worker groups (Indian, Pakistani, Filipino workers in Gulf states)
2. Create an official "Email Remittance Pro" Telegram channel
3. Join crypto/DAO channels and engage in payout discussions
4. Share infographics and video content
5. Run AMA sessions in relevant groups

### Viber (Philippines-specific)

**Why:** Viber is the #1 messaging app in the Philippines, used heavily by OFW families.

**Tactics:**
1. Join OFW family Viber groups
2. Share Tagalog-language content
3. Partner with OFW community leaders on Viber

## B2B Channels

### Governance Forums (DAOs)

**Tactics:**
1. Write formal proposals for DAO governance (see outreach_engine.py `dao_proposal` template)
2. Engage with existing payout/treasury discussions
3. Build reputation by contributing thoughtfully before proposing
4. Provide technical documentation showing integration simplicity
5. Offer pilot program: 10 test transactions, free

**Targets:** Uniswap, Arbitrum, Optimism, Gitcoin, Coordinape, Aave, Compound

### LinkedIn (B2B Payroll/HR platforms)

**Tactics:**
1. Connect with partnership/BD leads at target companies
2. Share thought leadership: "The future of global payroll is email + gift cards"
3. Engage with HR/payroll industry content
4. InMail outreach to decision-makers with ROI calculator

### Email (B2B cold outreach)

**Tactics:**
1. Use templates from outreach_engine.py (`b2b_payroll`, `freelance_platform`)
2. Personalize first line with specific company pain point
3. Follow up sequence: Day 0 → Day 3 → Day 7 → Day 14
4. Include one-pager PDF attachment showing API integration
5. Track opens/replies via outreach tracking

### Conferences & Events

**Tactics:**
1. Attend fintech/crypto conferences: ETHDenver, Consensus, Money2020
2. Host side events or workshops on "Crypto remittance without friction"
3. Distribute one-pagers and demo QR codes
4. Collect leads via QR code → landing page → email signup

## Content Marketing Channels

### SEO Blog Content

**Target keywords (by corridor):**
- "how to send money to Mexico" (90K/mo searches)
- "cheapest way to send money to Philippines" (40K/mo)
- "send money to India from UAE" (30K/mo)
- "crypto remittance" (12K/mo)
- "send gift card to Mexico" (8K/mo)

**Content strategy:**
1. Create comparison articles: "Western Union vs Email Remittance Pro (2026)"
2. How-to guides: "How to Send Money to Philippines Instantly"
3. Cost calculator pages: interactive fee comparison tools
4. Corridor-specific landing pages optimized for local SEO

### YouTube

**Content types:**
1. Demo walkthroughs (3-5 min)
2. Fee comparison explainers
3. Testimonial compilations
4. "How it works" animated explainers
5. Live send/claim demonstrations

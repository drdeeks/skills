## Tier 1 Corridors

### US → Mexico ($63B/year)

**Why first:** Largest single corridor, highest absolute volume, strong gift card infrastructure.

**Audience profile:**
- 12M+ Mexican-born residents in US (Pew, 2024)
- Average remittance: $200/month
- Recipient demographics: 40% rural, 60% urban, 35% elderly
- Key states: TX (3.2M), CA (4.1M), AZ (600K), IL (800K)

**Channel strategy:**
| Channel | Tactic | Target metric |
|---------|--------|---------------|
| Facebook Groups | Join "Mexicanos en USA" (500K+), "Paisanos en California" | 50 group engagements/week |
| WhatsApp | Seed in worker network chains (construction, restaurants) | 20 groups joined/month |
| TikTok | 30s demo videos in Spanish showing fee comparison | 10K views/month |
| Instagram | Testimonial carousels, before/after fee comparisons | 5K impressions/week |
| Churches | Partner with Hispanic church community leaders | 5 church partnerships |
| Community events | Sponsor Mexican Independence Day, Cinco de Mayo events | 3 events/quarter |

**Gift cards:** Walmart Mexico, Soriana, Oxxo, Coppel, Liverpool, Amazon Mexico

**Messaging angle:** "Envia mas, paga menos. 1.5% — no 8%. Tu familia elige como recibir."

**Competitive displacement targets:** Western Union (8-12%), MoneyGram (5-8%), Remitly (3-5%)

### UAE → India ($20B/year)

**Why second:** Dense expat worker concentration, high smartphone penetration, gift card culture.

**Audience profile:**
- 3.4M Indian nationals in UAE
- 60% blue-collar (construction, service), 40% white-collar
- Average remittance: $150/month (blue-collar), $400/month (white-collar)
- Key cities: Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah

**Channel strategy:**
| Channel | Tactic | Target metric |
|---------|--------|---------------|
| Telegram | Indian worker community groups in Dubai/Abu Dhabi | 30 groups/month |
| WhatsApp | Labor camp welfare officer partnerships | 10 camps/month |
| Facebook | NRI groups, "Indians in Dubai" pages | 20 pages engaged |
| YouTube | Hindi/Malayalam demo videos | 5K views/month |
| Cultural events | Diwali, Eid, Onam celebrations — live demos | 4 events/quarter |
| Salary day activations | Distribute flyers at labor accommodation on 25th-1st | 5K flyers/month |

**Gift cards:** Amazon India, Flipkart, Big Bazaar, Reliance Digital, Jio recharge

**Messaging angle:** "Ghar paise bhejo, sirf 1.5% fees. Email se. Mummy ko Amazon card milega 30 second mein."

### US → Philippines ($18B/year)

**Audience profile:**
- 4M+ Filipino-Americans
- Top occupations: nursing (500K+), caregiving, maritime, domestic work
- Average remittance: $180/month
- Strong community organization (PNA chapters, OFW groups)

**Channel strategy:**
| Channel | Tactic | Target metric |
|---------|--------|---------------|
| Facebook | OFW groups (millions of members), PNA chapter pages | 30 groups/month |
| Viber | Filipino community group chats | 15 groups/month |
| Email | Professional association newsletters (PNA, PNAA) | 5 newsletter placements |
| TikTok | Tagalog demo videos, "remittance hack" content | 8K views/month |
| Podcasts | Guest spots on Filipino-American podcasts | 2 appearances/quarter |

**Gift cards:** SM Store, Robinson's, Puregold, Globe/Smart Load, Shopee PH

**Messaging angle:** "Magpadala ng pera sa pamilya mo — 1.5% lang. Email lang kailangan. Gift card o crypto, sila ang pipili."

## Tier 2 Corridors

### Saudi → Pakistan ($8B/year)
- 2.5M Pakistani workers in Saudi Arabia
- Channels: WhatsApp (dominant), Facebook, YouTube
- Gift cards: Daraz, Carrefour Pakistan, Foodpanda
- Language: Urdu
- Key dates: Eid al-Fitr, Eid al-Adha (peak remittance periods)

### UK → Nigeria ($6.5B/year)
- 250K+ Nigerian diaspora in UK (high-value senders)
- Channels: Twitter/X (Nigerian Twitter is massive), WhatsApp, Instagram
- Gift cards: Jumia Nigeria, Shoprite, MTN/Glo airtime
- Language: English, Pidgin
- Highest incumbent fees (7.1%) = biggest displacement opportunity

### Canada → India ($5B/year)
- 1.8M Indian-Canadians
- Channels: WhatsApp, Facebook, LinkedIn (professional diaspora)
- Gift cards: Amazon India, Flipkart
- Language: English, Hindi, Punjabi

## Corridor Launch Checklist

For each new corridor activation:

- [ ] Identify top 3 gift card providers in destination country
- [ ] Verify Tango Card / Reloadly coverage for those gift cards
- [ ] Translate claim page to destination language
- [ ] Create 3 outreach templates (community, influencer, SEO)
- [ ] Identify 20+ community groups/pages to engage
- [ ] Find 5+ micro-influencers (5K-50K followers) in the corridor
- [ ] Create fee comparison graphic vs top 3 competitors in corridor
- [ ] Set up corridor-specific tracking in analytics
- [ ] Translate email notifications to destination language
- [ ] Test end-to-end flow with destination gift cards

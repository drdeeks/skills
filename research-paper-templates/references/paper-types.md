# Research Paper Templates - Paper Types

## Empirical ML Papers
Standard structure: Abstract, Introduction, Related Work, Methods, Experiments, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, Limitations, Appendix

## Theory Papers
Focus on: Problem formulation, Theoretical contributions, Proofs (in appendix), Experimental validation (if applicable)

## Survey Papers
Focus on: Taxonomy, Comprehensive coverage, Gap analysis, Future directions

## Benchmark Papers
Focus on: Dataset description, Evaluation protocol, Baseline results, Leaderboard

## Position Papers
Focus on: Argument, Evidence, Counter-arguments, Community impact

## Reproducibility Papers
Focus on: Reproduction study, Findings, Discrepancies, Recommendations
